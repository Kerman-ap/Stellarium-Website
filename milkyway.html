The Milky Way

What is the milky way galaxy?
The Milky Way galaxy is a barred spiral galaxy that contains over 100 billion stars, including our own sun. It is estimated to be about 100,000 light-years in diameter and is located about 25,000 light-years away from the center of the galaxy. The Milky Way is also home to numerous other celestial objects such as:
  • Planets
  • Asterioids
  • Comets
  • Black Holes
